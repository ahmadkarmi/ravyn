# Ravyn App Store Description

**Title:** Ravyn
**Subtitle:** Lock in priorities. Build streaks. Execute daily.

## Description

Ravyn is not just another endless to-do list. It's a daily execution system designed to help you lock in your focus and actually get things done.

Most task managers become overwhelming graveyards of postponed ideas. Ravyn forces you to commit to what matters today, builds your momentum through streaks, and uses an Integrity System to keep you honest with your own schedule.

**🔥 Built for Momentum**
- **The Daily Slate:** Focus only on what’s due today. Add context, priority, and tags.
- **Streaks & Boosts:** Build a daily streak by closing tasks. Earn "Boosts" to protect your streak when life inevitably gets in the way.
- **Integrity Points:** Earning points is simple: close tasks on time. Rescheduling or missing tasks will penalize you. Stay honest, earn points, and unlock Boosts.

**⚡ Frictionless Workflow**
- **Smart Reminders:** Plan your morning, get nudged midday if you're falling behind, and wrap up your day before the window shuts.
- **Swipe Actions:** Instantly close, edit, or reschedule tasks without opening menus.
- **Tagging & Search:** Keep your work organized with color-coded tags and instantly find what you need.

**🔒 Secure & Private**
- **Offline First:** Your tasks are instantly saved locally. You don't need a connection to execute.
- **Cloud Sync:** Securely sync your data across devices with a private account.
- **Your Data:** Export all your tasks and records at any time directly from your profile.

Stop planning. Start executing. Download Ravyn and own your day.
