# Privacy Policy for Ravyn

**Last Updated:** February 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Ravyn ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and an optional display name.
- **Task Data:** We collect your tasks, tags, daily records, and integrity points. This data is synced to our secure cloud database (Supabase) to provide cross-device access.
- **Local Data:** The App stores data locally on your device for offline use.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality.
- To sync your data across devices.
- To send local push notifications (daily reminders) which are scheduled directly on your device.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)
Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar laws, you have the right to:
- **Access Your Data:** You can export all your tasks, tags, and stats at any time using the "Export My Data" feature in the Profile tab.
- **Delete Your Data:** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data using the "Delete Account" feature in the Profile tab. This action cannot be undone.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use Supabase for authentication and database hosting. We use Sentry for crash reporting to improve app stability. These services process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at support@ravyn.app.
