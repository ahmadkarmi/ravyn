Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

Ravyn is a personal commitment tracker. The entire point of this app is to help you close the things that matter. We have no interest in your data beyond making that work well. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

1. Who We Are

Ravyn is an independently developed and operated app. Throughout this policy, "we," "us," and "our" refer to the Ravyn team. "You" and "your" refer to you, the person using the app.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at: privacy@ravyn.app

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect what is necessary to operate the app.

Category What it includes Why we collect it
Account data Email address, hashed password (if you use email sign-in), or OAuth profile ID (if you use Google sign-in) To authenticate you and sync your data across devices
Task data Task titles, descriptions, due dates, status, tags, and close timestamps Core app functionality
Progress data Streak count, integrity points, boost tokens, daily records To calculate and display your momentum over time
App settings Start and end of day times, notification preferences, theme choice To personalise the experience and deliver timely reminders
Crash and error data Anonymous crash reports including device type, OS version, app version, and stack traces. No personal task content is included. To fix bugs and improve stability

We do not collect: your location, contacts, camera, microphone, payment information, or any data from other apps on your device. Photos are only accessed if you explicitly choose to set a profile picture.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the app and sync your tasks and progress across your devices
  • To send local push notifications you have opted into (reminders are scheduled on-device and never sent through our servers)
  • To calculate streaks, integrity points, and boost tokens
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs using anonymous error reports
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving Ravyn.

4. Third-Party Services

Ravyn uses a small number of carefully chosen third-party services.

Service Purpose Data shared Privacy policy
Supabase Database, authentication, and cloud storage Account data, tasks, tags, progress data, settings supabase.com/privacy
Sentry Crash and error reporting Anonymous crash reports, device info, app version, a pseudonymous user ID. No task content or personal details. sentry.io/privacy
Google Optional OAuth sign-in If you choose "Continue with Google," Google shares your email address and profile ID with us. No other Google data is accessed. policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell your data to any third party. We do not use data brokers. We do not have advertising partners.

5. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device - Ravyn caches your tasks and settings locally so the app works without an internet connection.
  • In the cloud - If you create an account, your data is synced to Supabase servers. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest.

If you use Ravyn without an account, your data stays on your device only and is never sent to our servers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all of your data - tasks, tags, progress records, and account credentials - is permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours. Device-local data is cleared immediately at the point of deletion.

Anonymous crash reports are retained by Sentry for 90 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data. We respect these rights for all users regardless of location.

Right How to exercise it
Access and portability Go to Profile > Data & Legal > Export My Data. You will receive a full JSON export of your tasks, tags, and stats.
Deletion (right to be forgotten) Go to Profile > Account > Delete Account. This permanently and irreversibly deletes all your data.
Correction You can edit your tasks, tags, and display name directly within the app at any time.
Objection or restriction Contact us at privacy@ravyn.app and we will respond within 30 days.
Withdraw consent You can disable push notifications at any time in Profile > Settings, or in your device's system settings.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

Ravyn is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@ravyn.app and we will delete it promptly.

9. Push Notifications

Ravyn uses local push notifications to remind you of tasks and your daily closing window. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not require sending your data to our servers. You can grant or revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings. Revoking permission does not affect any other part of the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top and, where required by law, notify you directly via the app or email. Continued use of Ravyn after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
