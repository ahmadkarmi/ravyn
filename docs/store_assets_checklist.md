# App Store & Google Play Asset Checklist

To submit Ravyn to both stores, you will need to create the following graphical and textual assets. 

## 1. App Icons
*The icon users see on their home screen and in the store.*

**Apple App Store (iOS)**
- **Dimensions:** 1024 x 1024 pixels
- **Format:** PNG (no transparency/alpha channel)
- **Safe Area:** Apple automatically applies the rounded corners. Do not round the corners yourself.

**Google Play Store (Android)**
- **Dimensions:** 512 x 512 pixels
- **Format:** 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
- **File Size:** Up to 1MB
- **Safe Area:** Google Play automatically applies rounding and drop shadows.

---

## 2. Screenshots
*Images showcasing the app's UI and features.*

**Apple App Store (iOS)**
*You need a minimum of 1, but 3-5 is recommended. You must provide them in two specific sizes.*
- **6.5" Display (iPhone 11 Pro Max, 12 Pro Max, 13 Pro Max, 14 Plus):** 1284 x 2778 pixels (Portrait)
- **5.5" Display (iPhone 8 Plus, 7 Plus, 6s Plus):** 1242 x 2208 pixels (Portrait)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG (no transparency)
- **Note:** Apple rejects screenshots that include the iPhone notch/Dynamic Island in the design. Just use clean, framed UI shots or raw device outputs.

**Google Play Store (Android)**
*You need a minimum of 2, maximum of 8.*
- **Dimensions:** Between 320px and 3840px (e.g., 1080 x 1920 pixels is standard for portrait). The maximum dimension cannot be more than twice as long as the minimum dimension.
- **Format:** JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- **File Size:** Up to 8MB

---

## 3. Feature Graphic / Promotional Image
*Used prominently in the Google Play Store (and sometimes App Store if featured).*

**Google Play Store (Required)**
- **Dimensions:** 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format:** JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- **Safe Area:** Keep text and logos in the center to avoid cropping.

**Apple App Store (Optional)**
- Only required if your app is being featured by Apple editorially (4320 x 1080 pixels). Not needed for initial launch.

---

## 4. Text & URLs
*The written content for your store listings. (I've already drafted the Description and Release Notes in `app_store_copy.md`).*

**Both Stores**
- **App Name:** Ravyn (Max 30 chars for iOS, 50 chars for Android)
- **Subtitle / Short Description:** Lock in priorities. Build streaks. Execute daily. (Max 30 chars iOS, 80 chars Android)
- **Promotional Text (iOS only):** (Max 170 chars) - *Optional, appears above the description.*
- **Keywords (iOS only):** (Max 100 chars, comma separated) e.g., `todo,task manager,productivity,streak,habit tracker,daily planner,adhd,focus`
- **Support URL:** e.g., `https://ravyn.app/support` or your email `mailto:support@ravyn.app`
- **Privacy Policy URL:** e.g., `https://ravyn.app/privacy` (Required by both stores)
- **Copyright:** e.g., `© 2026 Ravyn`

---

## Tips for Creating the Assets
1. **Use a Generator:** Tools like [Previewed.app](https://previewed.app), [AppMockUp](https://appmockup.com), or [Rotato](https://rotato.app) can take your raw screenshots and instantly generate properly sized, framed, and text-captioned App Store/Google Play screenshot sets.
2. **Focus on the Hooks:** Make your first 2 screenshots the most compelling (e.g., "Build your daily streak" or "The Integrity System"). Most users do not scroll past the second screenshot.
